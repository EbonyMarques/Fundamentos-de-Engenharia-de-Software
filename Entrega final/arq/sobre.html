About the platform


Protechted is a tool to support beach goers to monitor hazards and warn of precautionary measures,
which alerts you about important details about the place to be visited before you even arrive and while you are there.



Features


In addition to displaying climate and temperature-related data and interpreting the uv index of any beach
in the database in real time, Protechted allows users to co-operate to exchange experiences and, above all, protected fun.
